Hellfreeze - the Curse of the Ice Queen

The land of the north - Behlgard - is in chaos. Hell has frozen over and no evil creature can die. Even the Devil himself is caged in ice. All of this was done by a legendary curse cast by the wicked Ice Queen Zirla.
The remaining forces of Behlgard had rallied behind King Gallowan to destroy Zirla once and for all before she could conqure the whole north. But the King did not know of the ice curse and every creature of Zirla they brought down in the battle rose up again.
The King lost almost half of his forces before they had a chance to call for retreat. Now preassured in his last fort of defence King Gallowan must send his firstborn - You - to Hell with the mission to free the Devil and make hell the inferno it once was. And then send Zirla and her evil creatures there forever.
Now go forth, free the Devil and bring hope back to Behlgard!
